About me

I'm a fourth-year student of the Technical Gymnasium program at Vegova Ljubljana where I'm also a member of the Vegovacraft club.

Outside the classroom, my passion lies in the realm of programming, especially web development. In 2019, I first found out how to make web sites using a book from a library. I liked it, so I started learning web development and I've been hooked ever since. I'm currently learning and making projects with ReactJS, NodeJS and sometimes ESP32 microcontrollers.

When I'm not coding, I like to fly my drone (DJI Mini 2 SE), play video games and fiddle with electronics. I also like to photograph and edit photos, as well as make videos.

My skills

I make my projects mainly with ReactJS with ViteJS or just vanilla HTML, CSS and JS. I am experienced with Git, Github and programming Arduino and ESP32 microcontrollers in C++ using PlatformIO.

Apart from programming, I'm an A1/A3 drone pilot and also an amateur radio operator with callsign [not yet].
